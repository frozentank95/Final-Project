Column Explanation

  • This research employed a binary variable, default payment (Yes = 1, No = 0), as the response variable. This study reviewed the literature and used the following 23 variables as explanatory variables:
  • Given Amount Credit : Amount of the given credit (NT dollar): it includes both the individual consumer credit and his/her family (supplementary) credit
  • Gender : (1 = male; 2 = female)
  • Education : (1 = graduate school; 2 = university; 3 = high school; 4 = others)
  • Marital status : (1 = married; 2 = single; 3 = others)
  • Age in year
  • Repayment Sep - Jul: History of past payment. 0 = pay duly, 1 = payment late 1 month, 2 = payment late 2 month, continue
  • Amount of Bill Sep - Jul: Amount of bill statement (NT dollar)
  • Amount Prev Pay Sep - Jul: Amount of previous payment (NT dollar)
  • Total Bill : Total Amount of Bill from past 6 months
  • Total Pay : Total Amount of Pay from prast 6 months
  • Mean Pay Late : Mean Late Payment per month
  • Count Late : Total Count Late
  • Debt : Total Amount Debt (Bill - Payment)
